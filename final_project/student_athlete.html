Being A Student Athlete In High School

Tip 1:
STUDENT comes first in student athlete, so school should come first.
Just do what you got to do in school.

Tip 2:
It may be hard to manage time. An easy solution is to make meal time,
friends time. So your saving the rest of your time for the sport
you play and school.

Tip 3:
Give it yor all, every single day. Do ever play less than your best.
Most impportantly have FUN!
