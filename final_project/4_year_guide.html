Freshman Year:
This is the year where you shoud observe your surrounding.
Freshman Friday is not real, so don't miss the first friday of the school year.
The most important thing to do this school year is be yourself.

Sophmore Year:
This school year you may think everyone is you friends, but that is not the case.
Make sure you know the differences between friends and associates.

Junior Year:
This year is where you really have to focus.
If you work really hard now, you can live it up your senior year without
much worry about your school work.

Senior Year:
Make It Count. This year is the year you definitely want to end on a good note.
Do What You Got To Do, But Especially Have FUN!
