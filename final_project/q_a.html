Q/A Page

Questions Asked

How to make friends?
Making friends is easy. You'll do fine if you just be yourself and have an
open mindset.

Is the work hard?
No, it's not that hard just focus when the teacher is teaching and you'll do fine.

Are high schools like the movies?
In some ways, high school is like the movies. In other ways, it isn't.
High schools don't look like the high schools in the movies. However, the
moments that happen in high school should be valued just like the characters
in the movie values them. They are lessons that shape you ask a person.
