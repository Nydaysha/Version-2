Self Esteem

Tip 1:
Know Your Self-Worth.
If you feel like someone isn't good enough for you, then it is ok to walk away.

Tip 2:
Be Yourself. Don't sink in to peer pressure.
Don't give anyone the power to make you think you have to change yourself.
The only way you should change yourself is if you truly believe you
are benefiting from the change.
